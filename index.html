关于我 About Me

我是一名充满热情的城市设计师和建筑师，目前在香港大学攻读城市设计硕士学位。我的研究兴趣集中在城市空间分析、交通安全与建成环境的关系，以及数据驱动的城市规划方法。

在学术研究中，我运用深度学习、空间分析和统计建模等技术，探索城市环境对人类行为的影响。同时，我也积极参与实际项目，从建筑设计到城市规划，从品牌视觉到新媒体运营，力求在理论与实践之间找到平衡。

我相信好的设计能够改善人们的生活质量，而数据科学为我们提供了更精准的洞察。我致力于将这两者结合，为创造更美好的城市环境贡献力量。

I am a passionate urban designer and architect currently pursuing a Master of Urban Design at the University of Hong Kong. My research interests focus on urban spatial analysis, the relationship between traffic safety and built environment, and data-driven urban planning approaches.

In my academic research, I employ deep learning, spatial analysis, and statistical modeling to explore how urban environments influence human behavior. Meanwhile, I actively participate in practical projects, ranging from architectural design to urban planning, from brand visuals to new media operations, seeking balance between theory and practice.

I believe good design can improve people's quality of life, while data science provides us with more precise insights. I am committed to combining these two aspects to contribute to creating better urban environments.
